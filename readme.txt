=========================================
      BIZLAUNCH WORK MODEL
=========================================

BizLaunch is a manager for web development businesses. 
Our work model is designed to help you find, manage, and close 
clients who need a professional website.

1. FIND (Prospect Finder)
   - Search for businesses using Google Maps.
   - Filter by high ratings (4.0+) and "No Website" status.
   - We target businesses that are successful but invisible online.

2. ANALYZE (Audit Tools)
   - Run a quick audit on a business's current digital presence.
   - Identify gaps: Mobile issues, slow speed, or missing SEO.
   - Generate points to show the owner exactly what they are missing.

3. PROPOSE (Proposal Gen)
   - Create professional, high-value proposals in seconds.
   - Show the client the "Opportunity" (the money they are losing).
   - Use our templates for Premium Websites or SEO Refreshes.

4. MANAGE (CRM & Dashboard)
   - Track your leads from "Found" to "Contract Signed."
   - See your stats: Total Prospects, Active Clients, and Deal Sizes.
   - Scale your business by keeping your pipeline full.
